Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (2001)

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (also known as Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone in the United States) is a movie of the fantasy genre, released in 2001 across the globe, based on the 1997 novel of th same name by J.K. Rowling. It was directed by Chris Colombus, also known for 'Home Alone' (1980) and 'Home Alone 2: Lost in New York' (1992).


Cast

The movie stars Daniel Radcliffe as the young Harry Potter, the lead role, supported by his friends Emma Watson as Hermione Granger and Rupert Grint as Ron Weasley.
